Christmas Market Hidden Object

Find Hidden Objects Fast

A cozy Christmas adventure in every scene

Step into a magical holiday world filled with festive market stalls, twinkling lights, and cleverly tucked-away items. Christmas Market Hidden Object challenges your observation skills as you hunt for listed objects before the timer runs out.

Explore richly detailed Christmas environments, unlock new levels, earn stars for speed and accuracy, and enjoy a smooth, relaxing pace—easy to pick up, satisfying to master. Compete on Game Center leaderboards and see how sharp your eye really is.

How to play

  1. Open a level and read the objects you need to find.
  2. Scan the festive scene and tap each hidden item when you spot it.
  3. Beat the clock to finish the level and collect stars.
  4. Progress through unique holiday-themed scenes and aim for a perfect run.
